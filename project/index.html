Hostel Facility

Exhilarated hostel facility is offered for students at Axis Colleges with the capacity of 300 students in boy’s hostel and 300 students in girl’s hostel with conducive and homely environment. The hostlers enjoy comfortable stay, nutritious food and round the clock security to concentrate on their studies. Hostel also gives students opportunities to manage with peers from different backgrounds in harmonious manner. All rooms are bestowing with good lighting and sufficient ventilation with air cooling/conditioning facilities during summer.

Facilities Common Room— All hostels have a common room used for recreation purposes. Daily newspaper and magazines are kept for the benefits of hostlers. Satellite cable Television facility is also available. 24×7 Wi-Fi facility is available to hostlers.
